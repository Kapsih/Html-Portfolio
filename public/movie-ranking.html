The Best Movies According To Anish

My Top 3 Movies of all-time.

Ee Nagaraniki Emaindi

One of the best coming of the age films. The film is about filmmaking and friendship crafted with really good humour.

Available on Netflix.

Language: Telugu.

Kirik Party

Again a coming of age film and once again set in an engineering college. Shot with great style and flawless acting by the lead-Rakshit Shetty.

Available on Jio Cinemas.

Language: Kannada.

Pulp Fiction

A film about the crime that chooses not to be melodramatic, infact it is written and shot in an quirky way. A film that you'll love if you pay close attention to the dialogues.

Available on Amazon Prime.

Language: English.
